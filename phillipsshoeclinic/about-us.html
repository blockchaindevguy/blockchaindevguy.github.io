Who we are

Phillip's Shoe Clinic has been operating in Chicago's North Shore for over 100 years.  Mr. Phillip retired after 60 years of amazing service and he left the business to Oscar. As the second generation in the company, we continue to follow the same ​principles of when the business began - serving customers with the same kindness, respect and ensuring that each customer leaves satisfied.



​Meet the Owner
​
Oscar is originally from Mexico City and is also a family man; married to a beautiful wife and father of two. He has over 20 years of experience in the shoe repair industry and is an expert and in charge of the area of sewing and care to all type of leather. ​
